## Dashboard Views

1. **KPI Summary Strip** — Trial-to-paid rate, avg days to convert, top activation feature, MRR
2. **Conversion Funnel** — Stage-by-stage drop-off with conversion percentages
3. **Cohort Retention Heatmap** — Monthly cohorts × months since signup, color-coded retention %
4. **Feature Adoption vs. Conversion** — Which features predict conversion
5. **Revenue by Plan Tier** — MRR breakdown, ARPU, churn rate per tier
