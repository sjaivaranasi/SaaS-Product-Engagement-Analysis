## Dashboard Views

1. **KPI Summary Strip** — Trial-to-paid rate, avg days to convert, top activation feature, MRR
2. **Conversion Funnel** — Horizontal bar showing drop-off at each stage
3. **Cohort Retention Heatmap** — Months × cohorts, color-coded retention %
4. **Feature Adoption vs. Conversion** — Grouped bar comparing churned vs. active usage

| Role | Hex | Usage |
